# Camp 404 — Project Brief
*Cross-platform camp management application for the Afrikaburn theme camp "Camp 404"*

**Domain:** camp-404.com (may swap to camp-404.net)
**Document version:** 0.2 — May 19, 2026

---

## 1. Executive Summary / Project Vision

Camp 404 is a long-running Afrikaburn theme camp (founded 2019, ~40 international members) that gifts radical hospitality to the Tankwa Karoo: 24/7 vegan snacks, daily vegan brunch and dinners, a safe space for "lost burners," the Now Now Meow Meow mutant vehicle, LED art pieces, workshops, and "The Dance of 1000 Flames" — the largest fire jam at Afrikaburn, held immediately before the Clan burns on Saturday night. Each year the camp also adopts up to five stranded burners (the camp's "Orphanage" shadow gift), provisioning them with tent, bedding, and fridge space.

The **Camp 404 app** ("the app") is a cross-platform tool — web + iOS + Android, all from a single codebase — to run the camp's operations end-to-end: member sign-up and payment, dietary-aware meal planning with AI recipe analysis, voice-driven updates from the dust, photo-and-voice manual generation, reimbursement tracking, team-based push notifications, and the logistics of adoption, workshops, art, and fire performance.

The vision is **"a calm command centre for a chaotic desert."** It must work for a non-technical Camp Lead in Cape Town in February, a fire-performance coordinator in the desert in April with intermittent signal, and a German member submitting a recipe by voice from Berlin in March. The app is *not* a social network; it is an internal operations tool used by ~30–80 people once a year.
